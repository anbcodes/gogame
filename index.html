GOGAME


Gogame is a game where all blocks are in the shape of a planet.
You can download it here


You will need to unzip the file then double click on the gogame.exe (or just gogame)


How to use:

  • Use WASD to move
  • Use left click to dig
  • Use right click to place
  • Use 1234567890-= to change hotbar slots
  • Use E to open your inventory and then hover over the material then press the hotbar key
